    What is the Array of Things?

    The Array of Things (AoT) is a an urban measurement project that aims to collect data on local environments for community and research use.

    How is data measured?

    Nodes located in high traffic areas mornitor factors that impact pollution or livability, such as environmental, air quality, and light/infrared. Three sensors around Vanderbilt campus record these values and store the data on the Vanderbilt AoT database.

    Our goal is to design a dashboard for the Array of Things that displays data collected by the sensors in a way that is digestible for the general publix.

    Although the AoT data is already made available free of charge, trends in the information may be difficult to spot in the raw data.
